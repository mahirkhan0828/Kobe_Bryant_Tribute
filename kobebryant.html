Kobe Timeline:

  • 1978-Born in Philedelphia, father is Joe (also known as "Jelly Bean") Bryant and was a 76ers player
  • 1996-Lakers trade Divac in order to get the 13th pick and draft 17 year old Bryant, coming straight out of Highschool
  • 1997-1996-Kobe named to all rookie team, averaging 7.6 points off the bench
  • 1999-00-Bryant wins first championship with teammate Shaquille O'neal and coach Phil Jackson, against the Pacers
  • 2000-01-Kobe records his first 50 point game and averages 28.5 points per game. Lakers go on to repeat as champions, beating the 76ers
  • 2001-02-Gets All Star MVP and the Lakers Three Peat, sweeping the Nets in the finals.
  • 2003-04-Kobe and Shaq loses in the finals to Detroit. Duo is broken up with Shaq traded
  • 2004-05-Lakers have terrible season with new coach quitting in the mid-season while Bryant suffers from injuries
  • 2005-06- Bryant gets scoring title with 31.6 points per game but Lakers lose in first round of playoffs. Kobe is frustrated and wants to leave Lakers.
  • 2007-08-Lakers are able to keep Bryant and make midseason trade for All Star Pau Gasol. Kobe wins MVP award but Lakers fall short to the Celtics in the finals
  • 2008-09-Bryant wins fourth nba title and Finals MVP against the Orlando Magic
  • 2009-10-Kobe wins fifth nba title with Finals MVP as the Lakers take the Celtics to 7 games in the finals
  • 2012-13-Kobe gets his 15th all star appearance and passes Wilt Chamberlain on all time scoring list. He also ruptures his achilles tendon, causing him to miss many games
  • 2016-Lakers hold a farewell game for Kobe as he drops 60 points with the whole world watching. In attendance are his former teammates and many celebrities
  • 2018-Kobe wins academy award for his short film Dear Basketball
  • 2020- Kobe, daughter Gianna, and her teammates pass away in tragic helicopter accident while flying out for the team\'s game
